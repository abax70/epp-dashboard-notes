Initial Employment Employment rates for EPP completers in North Carolina public schools

Overview

This dashboard tracks how many candidates who completed their EPP go on to teach in North Carolina public schools (including charter schools). You can view employment rates within one, two, or three years of program completion.

Who Is Included

Candidates pursuing initial teaching licenses (see Glossary) who completed their educator preparation program. The dashboard covers completers from the most recent ten years.

Note on alternate-route candidates: By definition, candidates on alternate routes are employed in schools before completing their program. This dashboard does not count pre-completion employment—it picks up only after the candidate has completed.

Employment Windows

The dashboard offers three employment windows: within 1, 2, or 3 years of completion. Because longer windows require more time to elapse, the most recent completion cohorts may not yet appear for longer windows. For example, the 3-year window requires three full years of payroll data after completion, so the most recent eligible cohort will lag the current year accordingly.

Data Source

Candidate rosters are provided annually by EPPs to NCDPI. Employment data comes from NCDPI payroll files for North Carolina public schools.

Small Counts

There are no small-count suppression rules in effect for this dashboard. All groups are displayed regardless of size.
