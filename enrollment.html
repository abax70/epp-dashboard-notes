Enrollment Counts of candidates enrolled in North Carolina educator preparation programs

Overview

This dashboard shows counts of candidates enrolled in NC educator preparation programs (EPPs). It provides counts for candidates in three phases of enrollment:

  • Newly enrolled — candidates in their first year of enrollment
  • Completing — candidates who completed their program that year
  • Total enrolled — all candidates enrolled during the year, including those beginning, returning, and completing

Who Is Included

Candidates pursuing initial teaching licenses. Candidates seeking administrative licenses or additional teaching licenses are excluded. See the Glossary for more detail.

Degree Types

Enrollment counts can be disaggregated by degree type. EPPs offer programs at three levels:

Degree Type Description
Undergraduate Candidates enrolled in a bachelor’s degree program leading to initial teacher licensure.
Graduate Candidates enrolled in a master’s or other graduate-level program leading to initial teacher licensure. Includes Master of Arts in Teaching (MAT) and similar programs.
License Only Candidates who already hold a degree and are enrolled in a licensure-only pathway. Includes residency license holders and lateral entry candidates.

Data Source

EPPs submit annual rosters of enrolled candidates to NCDPI. These rosters are the underlying dataset for all of the EPP dashboards. The dashboard covers the most recent five years.

Small Counts

There are no small-count suppression rules for this dashboard.

Note: Enrollment counts from prior years may change. NCDPI works with EPPs throughout the year to clean the data and correct errors. Occasionally, a correction affects counts in both the current and prior years.
