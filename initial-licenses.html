Initial Licenses Counts of EPP candidates receiving their first teaching license in North Carolina

Overview

This dashboard shows the number of candidates who receive initial teaching licenses after completing an educator preparation program. It includes candidates on both traditional and alternate routes to licensure.

Note: By definition, alternate-route candidates receive their first license as part of their program, so they appear in this dashboard at the time of program enrollment rather than completion.

Who Is Included

Candidates receiving their first teaching license in the most recent five years. In their annual roster submissions to NCDPI, EPPs list up to three license areas each candidate is pursuing; these are used to determine whether a candidate received an initial teaching license.

Data Source

Candidate rosters are submitted annually by EPPs to NCDPI. Licensure data comes from NCDPI. See NC State Board of Education Policy LICN-001 for details on licensure requirements.

Small Counts

There is no minimum group size for this dashboard. All groups are displayed regardless of size.
